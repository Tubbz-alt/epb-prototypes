Find an energy assessment for a non-domestic building

Use this service to find energy assessments for non-domestic buildings in England, Wales and Northern Ireland.

Non-domestic buildings are commercial and public buildings.

For non-domestic buildings, you can view their:

  • energy performance certificate (EPC) and recommendations report (EPC-RR)
  • display energy certificate (DEC) and recommendation report (DEC-RR)
  • air conditioning inspection report (AC-REPORT) and certificate (AC-CERT)
This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
