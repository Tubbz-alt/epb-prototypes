Find an Energy Performance Certificate (EPC) for a domestic property

Use this service to find an EPC for a domestic property in England, Wales or Northern Ireland.

An EPC lists the energy rating of a property.

It also has advice on improving the energy performance of a property.

You need an EPC when you build, sell or let a domestic property. It is valid for 10 years.

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

Before you start

If you need additional support to find an EPC, call our helpdesk on 0233 788 1234 (Monday to Friday, 9am - 5pm).

For properties in Scotland, use the Scottish government's EPC service.

For commercial or public buildings, use the find a non-domestic EPC service.

To check if your property requires an EPC, read the guidance on properties that do not need an EPC.
