Find an energy assessor For your domestic property

Energy assessors measure the energy efficiency of your property and are qualified to issue you with an Energy Performance Certificate. You are required to have a recent Energy Performance Certificate before you market your property for sale or letting.

Who can use this service?

  • You are a UK home owner or you are representing one
  • You are selling or letting your house and need an Energy Performance Certificate
  • If your property is in Scotland, you need to use a different service
What is involved in an assessment
  • Access to the property must be provided to the assessor.
  • An assessment takes 30 minutes, depending on the size of your property.
  • It should not harm the property.
When you don't need an energy certificate
  • You are not selling or renting your property.
  • You are selling your property, but the buyer intends to demolish it.
  • The building is of less than 50m2 and not a dwelling.
  • The building is temporary with a planned time of use fewer than 2 years.

What you need to complete this service

  • The address of the property
  • A phone to contact assessors
