Find an energy assessor for a residential property

You need a recent Energy Performance Certificate (EPC) before you sell or let a residential property.

Use this service to find a qualified assessor to measure the energy efficiency of your property, or the property of someone you represent. The assessor will create an EPC.

You do not need an EPC if the property:
  • is less than 50m2
  • is not a residential property
  • is temporarily residential, with a planned use of 2 years or less
  • will be demolished
