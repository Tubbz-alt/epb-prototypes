Contact an accreditation schemes for energy assessors

A scheme is an entity outside of government. They accredit assessors in issuing Energy Performance Certification and other certifications.

When you should contact a scheme directly

  • You can't find an assessor
  • There was a problem with your assessor
  • You need to help someone without internet with their assessment
