Rules on letting this property

!
Warning

You may not be able to let this property

This building has an EPC rating of F or G. The landlord cannot grant a tenancy to new or existing tenants, unless an exemption has been registered.

From 1 April 2023, landlords will not be allowed to continue letting a non-domestic property which is already let if that building has an EPC rating of F or G.

You can read guidance for landlords on the regulations and exemptions.

Buildings can be let if they have an energy rating of A+ to E. The recommendations report sets out changes you can make to improve the building’s rating.


Energy efficiency rating for this building

This building’s current energy rating is F.

Buildings are given a rating from A+ (most efficient) to G (least efficient).

Buildings are also given a score. The larger the number, the more expensive your fuel bills are likely to be.
